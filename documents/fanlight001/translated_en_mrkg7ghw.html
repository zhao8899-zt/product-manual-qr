Fan Lamp Installation Guide

Product model: FL-2024-Pro

I. Preparation before installation

1. Please confirm that the accessories in the package are complete: 1 fan lamp body, 1 remote control, 4 expansion screws, 1 mounting bracket, 1 manual.

2. Before installation, please turn off the main power switch to ensure that the circuit has no power.

3. It is recommended that two people cooperate with the installation to ensure safety.

4. Preparation tools: Phillips screwdriver, electric drill, tape measure, pencil, insulated gloves.

II. Ceiling Installation Steps

1. Determine the installation position: Select a ceiling with a load-bearing capacity of not less than 30kg.

2. Mark the holes: Attach the mounting bracket to the ceiling and mark the 4 screw hole positions with a pencil.

3. Drilling: Use a 6mm electric drill to drill the hole at the marking position, with a depth of about 45mm.

4. Install the expansion screws: Insert the 4 expansion screws into the holes.

5. Fixing bracket: Screw the mounting bracket to the ceiling to ensure it is secure and not loose.

6. Wiring: Connect the power cord according to the wiring diagram (brown for fire wire L, blue for zero wire N, yellow-green for ground wire E).

7. Mounting body: Hang the fan lamp body on the mounting bracket and tighten the fixing screws.

8. Power-on test: After confirming that all connections are secure, turn on the main power switch for testing.

III. Wall Mount Installation Steps

1. Choose a solid wall with a load-bearing capacity of not less than 25kg.

2. Mark the hole position according to the wall mount bracket template.

3. Drill and install expansion screws.

4. Fix the wall mount, hang and lock the fan light.

IV. Remote control pairing

1. After powering on, press and hold the pairing button on the remote control for 3 seconds.

2. After hearing a beeping tone from the fan lamp, the pairing was successful.
