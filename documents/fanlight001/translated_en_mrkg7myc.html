Fan Lamp Instructions for Use

Product model: FL-2024-Pro

I. Product Overview

FL-2024-Pro Smart Fan Lamp is a smart home product that integrates lighting, fans, and ambient lights.Brushless DC motor, low noise, large air volume, energy saving and environmental protection.Support remote control and mobile app control to create a comfortable living space for you.

II. Product Parameters

Rated voltage: AC 220V 50Hz

Rated Power: Fan 36W + Lighting 24W = 60W

Fan speed: 3-speed adjustable (low/medium/high speed)

Air volume: max. 3500m3/h

Noise level: < = 35dB (low speed)

Illumination color temperature: 3000K-6500K adjustable (warm/natural/white)

Led lifetime: > = 50,000 hours

Product Dimensions: Diameter 560mm x Height 180mm

Net weight: 3.5kg

III. Function description of the remote control

1. Power button: short press on/off fan, long press on/off illumination for 3 seconds.

2. Wind speed key: cycle switch low/medium/high three gears.

3. Mode key: natural wind/sleep wind/strong wind three modes.

4. Light button: warm/natural/white light cycle switching.

5. Brightness key: illumination brightness stepless adjustment (press and hold gradient).

6. Timing key: 1 hour/2 hours/4 hours/8 hours timing off.

7. Reverse key: Fan forward and reverse switching (forward cooling in summer, reverse circulating heating in winter).

8. Pairing key: Pair with the fan light remotely.

IV. app Intelligent Control

1. Scan the QR code on the box to download the smart home app.

2. Sign up and log in.

3. Make sure your phone is connected to the 2.4GHz WiFi network.

4. Open the app, click Add Device and select Fan Lights.

5. Press and hold the remote control pairing key for 3 seconds and wait for the device to connect.
